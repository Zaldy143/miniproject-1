About Us



We are a private company based in the Philippines. We are a team of few so far but we are growing as we are expanding our reach outside the Philippines. We started this company to provide free services and job opportunities to people around the globe. BritzLink was born because of the founder's dream and vision. He imagines a world with better opportunites for those who are less previleged. He believes that with dedication and hardwork no task is hard.


The Founder


Mr. Rizaldy Britanico aspires to build an online company that allows the people to enjoy services for free but some other services are paid.. He also envisions that someday his company will be globally recognized and used. For him, achieving his goal is as difficult as finding a needle in a field of grains but with hardwork and dedication, nothing is impossible.


He's open to collaborate with small companies which need help with advertising platforms. He also envisions to provide thousands of jobs globally in the next 5 to 10 years.


I had the pleasure of working with the founder of BritzLink, and I can say without a doubt that he is a man of big dreams. He wants a better world for all of us, and he is constantly working towards that goal. He is an excellent leader and motivator, and I am proud to be a part of his team.
